About Me

My name is Jennifer Hoitenga and I am a student at Bellevue University. I have worked at a pediatric hospital for over 20 years. In 2001, I started in the Health Information Management department before moving to the Information Systems department in 2008. I accepted a database administrator role in 2013 and most recently, my position evolved to include custom programming including JavaScript, PHP, and APIs. In late 2022, I enrolled at Bellevue University to pursue a bachelor's degree in Web Development.


When I'm not working, I enjoy spending time with my dogs, Georgia and Olive. I also love gardening and visiting antique stores. I like finding unique vintage items and learning more about the history of the items I find.
